About me

Hi! My name is Samuel Yu-Hsiang Pan

I am an Ph.D. student in linguistics at National Tsing Hua University (NTHU, Taiwan).
My research interests include phonology and historical linguistics, with a special focus on Formosan languages. My M.A. thesis is about the phonology of Takibakha Bunun (卡社布農語).
